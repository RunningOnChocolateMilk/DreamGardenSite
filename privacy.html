DreamGarden Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your personal information

Effective Date: July 31st, 2025

At DreamGarden, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Apple Device Information: We receive limited device data that Apple provides to help the app function correctly (e.g., device type, OS version).
  • AI Interaction Data: When you use DreamGarden's AI chatbot, your input and the AI's responses are sent to and received from OpenAI's servers to provide gardening advice.

How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver and improve DreamGarden's features, including personalized AI gardening assistance.
  • To troubleshoot, analyze, and enhance app performance.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except to service providers necessary for app operation, such as OpenAI for AI functionality.
  • OpenAI processes your AI interaction data according to their privacy practices. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's privacy policy here: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.

Data Security

  • Your garden data is stored securely on your device using encrypted storage methods.
  • API keys and sensitive information are stored securely using Apple's Keychain services.

Your Rights

  • You may delete your garden data at any time via the app settings.
  • You can opt out of AI features by disabling them in the app.

Questions About This Policy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: hello@dreamgardenapp.com
