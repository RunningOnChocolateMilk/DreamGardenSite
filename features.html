🌱 DreamGarden – App Features

Discover how our AI-powered platform revolutionizes gardening in Australia

🤖

AI Gardening Assistant

Smart, friendly, and hyper-local advice tailored to your garden. Ask it anything — from pest problems to planting times.

  • Get instant answers to gardening questions
  • Receive personalized advice for your specific plants
  • Learn about Australian-native gardening techniques
🧠

Garden Memory System

Remembers everything: what you planted, where and when you planted it, and how it's growing over time. No more guessing or flipping through notebooks.

  • Track planting dates and locations
  • Monitor growth progress over time
  • Learn from your gardening history
🪴

Bed & Layout Planner

Visualise your garden beds and what's planted where. Plan ahead, track your rotations, and avoid overcrowding.

  • Design optimal garden layouts
  • Plan crop rotations effectively
  • Avoid common spacing mistakes
🌦️

Weather-Aware Advice

Local forecasts power smart notifications: fertiliser timing, rain-saving tips, and frost warnings. Your garden, tuned to the week ahead.

  • Get weather-based gardening tips
  • Receive frost and heat warnings
  • Optimize watering schedules
📆

Garden Calendar & Reminders

Custom tasks, feeding schedules, and seasonal to-dos — all in one timeline. Gentle nudges, not nagging alerts.

  • Never miss important garden tasks
  • Set custom reminders for your plants
  • Track seasonal gardening activities
🔒

Incredibly Strong Privacy

No trackers, no data selling, no surveillance. Your garden data stays yours — stored locally with strong encryption.

  • Zero data collection or tracking
  • Local storage with encryption
  • No third-party data sharing
  • Complete control over your information

Built by one passionate developer in Australia. Your support helps create better tools for gardeners, not corporate profits.

  • Support independent software development

  • Get direct developer support and feedback
