Mark your calendar - September 13, 2025!

After months of development, testing, and refinement, we're thrilled to announce that DreamGarden will officially launch on Saturday, September 13, 2025. This isn't just another gardening app – it's Australia's first AI-powered gardening companion designed specifically for our unique climate and growing conditions.

What to Expect on Launch Day

September 13, 2025, will be a celebration of Australian gardening innovation. Here's what you can look forward to:

What You'll Get on Launch Day

🤖 AI Gardening Assistant

Get personalized advice for Australian conditions, from pest problems to planting times

🧠 Garden Memory System

Never forget what you planted, where, and when. Track your garden's journey over time

📱 Bed Planner

Visualize your garden layout, plan rotations, and avoid overcrowding

🌤️ Weather Integration

Smart notifications based on local forecasts for optimal gardening decisions

How to Prepare for Launch

While you wait for September 13, here are some things you can do to get ready:

  • Join our waitlist to get notified the moment the app goes live
  • Follow us on social media for behind-the-scenes updates and gardening tips
  • Start planning your spring garden - DreamGarden will help you execute it perfectly
  • Take photos of your current garden to compare with your progress using the app
  • Research what you want to plant - the AI assistant will have answers to all your questions

What Makes DreamGarden Special

DreamGarden isn't just another gardening app. It's built specifically for Australian gardeners by someone who understands our unique challenges:

  • Local Knowledge: Designed for Australian climate, soil, and growing conditions
  • Privacy-First: Your garden data stays on your device with strong encryption
  • Independent Development: Built by one passionate developer, not a corporate team
  • Community Focused: Designed to help Australian gardeners succeed together
